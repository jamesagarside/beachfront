# Beachfront — Brand Feel

## The idea

Sandcastle is where the building happens — each repo, its own castle in the sand.
**Beachfront is the lookout over the whole shore.** It's the elevated, calm vantage
point from which an AI developer sees every sandcastle at once, and where the tide
of the backlog is. The product *is* a "single pane of glass" — so the brand is
literally about **clear sightlines over a coastline**.

The feeling to evoke: **calm command.** Not a noisy alerting dashboard — a quiet
promenade where, at a glance, you know what's handled and what needs you.

## Voice & tone

- **Calm and plainspoken.** Low drama. "12 repos calm · 3 need you" — never
  "⚠️ 3 CRITICAL ALERTS".
- **Observational, not bossy.** Beachfront surfaces; the human and the agents act.
- **Confident brevity.** Short labels, generous whitespace, no jargon walls.

## Colour — semantic by tide

Colour carries meaning, not decoration. Warm = needs a human; cool = calm/handled.

- **Sand** `#E9DCC3` — warm neutral background / surfaces
- **Deep sea** `#0B4F6C` — primary text / structure, the "glass frame"
- **Tide teal** `#1B998B` — healthy / fed / handled states (`ready-for-agent`, running)
- **Sky** `#A9D6E5` — secondary / info, calm fills
- **Sunrise coral** `#FF8C61` — **attention** accent, reserved for the Attention
  queue (`needs-triage`, `needs-human`) so "warm" always means "your turn"
- **Driftwood grey** `#8A8580` — idle / muted / dormant repos

Reserve coral strictly for "needs a human" — its scarcity is what makes the
Attention queue scannable.

## Typography

- **UI:** Inter (or system sans) — clean, neutral, dense-data friendly.
- **Wordmark/display:** a slightly characterful geometric sans for "Beachfront",
  evoking a horizon line. Lowercase, calm.

## Motif

A **horizon line** — the seam between sea and sky — as the recurring graphic
device: section dividers, the logo, empty states. The logo is a simple pane/window
framing a horizon, nodding to "pane of glass over the beachfront."

## Anti-patterns

- No red-alert klaxon styling; no badges screaming counts.
- No skeuomorphic sandcastles/buckets-and-spades kitsch — coastal *feel*, not a
  cartoon beach.
- No dense enterprise-dashboard chrome; favour air and a few strong signals.
